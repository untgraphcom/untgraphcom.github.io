Speakers

  • Dr. Sanjukta Bhowmick

    Associate Professor of Computer Science, University of North Texas, Denton, TX

    Dr. Sanjukta Bhowmick's research is on studying the properties of large, dynamic complex networks and using high performance computing to analyze them. I am particularly interested in understanding how noise affects network analysis, and developing uncertainty quantification for network analysis.

  • Dr. Yuede Ji

    Assistant Professor of Computer Science, University of North Texas, Denton, TX

    Dr. Yuede Ji directs the Graph Lab, which focuses on graph-centric security, learning, and computing.
